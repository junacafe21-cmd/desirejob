# How to Build an AI Telegram Agent with n8n (Chat & Auto-Upload)

If you want the bot to **ask you for missing information** (e.g., "The image doesn't mention the salary or deadline, what are they?"), you need to build a **Conversational AI Agent**. 

n8n has built-in "Advanced AI" nodes that make this incredibly easy. Instead of a linear script, you give the AI a "Memory" and a "Tool." The AI will talk to you on Telegram, inspect the image, realize what is missing, ask you for it, and then only use the "Tool" to save the job to Supabase once it has *all* the info.

---

## The AI Agent Architecture in n8n

### 1. The Chat Trigger & Memory
Instead of a normal trigger, we use the Conversational AI setup:
1. Add a **Telegram Trigger** node listening for `message`.
2. Attach a **Window Buffer Memory** node to the workflow so the AI Remembers your conversation history (so when it asks "What is the salary" and you reply "50k", it remembers what it asked).

### 2. The AI Agent Node
1. Add an **AI Agent** Node (Type: "Conversational Agent" or "ReAct Agent").
2. **Setup the Model**: Connect an **OpenAI Chat Model (gpt-4o)** to the Agent. Ensure "Vision" is enabled so it can see images.
3. **The System Prompt**: This is the magic. In the AI Agent's settings, give it this exact instruction:
   > *"You are the Desire Job Hub assistant. Your job is to upload job posters to the database. When the user sends an image, analyze it and extract: Title, Company, Location, Salary, Description, and Contact Info.*
   > 
   > *If ANY of these 6 pieces of information are missing from the poster, you MUST reply to the user in Telegram and ask them to provide the missing details (e.g., 'I see it is a Marketing job, but what is the Salary and Location?').*
   > 
   > *DO NOT call the 'SaveToSupabase' tool until you have gathered all 6 details. Once you have absolutely all 6, call the tool to save the job, and then tell the user 'The job has been successfully uploaded!'"*

### 3. The "Tool" (Saving to Supabase)
The AI needs a way to actually interact with your website database.
1. Create a **Custom Tool** node and attach it to the AI Agent.
2. Name the tool: `SaveToSupabase`.
3. Set the tool's input schema (what the AI needs to give it):
   - `title` (String)
   - `company` (String)
   - `location` (String)
   - `salary` (String)
   - `description` (String)
   - `contact_info` (String)
4. **Behind the Tool**: Connect a **Supabase Action** Node inside the custom tool configuration.
   - Resource: `Row`
   - Operation: `Insert`
   - Table: `jobs`
   - Data: Map the 6 properties the AI provides into your database columns using the n8n expression builder. Use your **Supabase Service Role Key** to bypass security policies.

### 4. Sending Messages Back
- You just need to connect the Output of the **AI Agent** back to a **Telegram Action** node.
- Operation: `Send Message`.
- Chat ID: `{{ $json.message.chat.id }}` (The person who sent the image).
- Text: `{{ $json.output }}` (The conversational response generated by the AI).

---

## Flow Summary
1. **You:** send a poster without a salary on Telegram.
2. **Telegram Trigger:** fires and hits the AI Agent.
3. **AI Agent:** Reads the prompt, looks at the image via OpenAI Vision, realizes the Salary is missing.
4. **Telegram Action:** Sends you a message: *"I extracted the title and company, but I can't find a Salary or Contact email. Please tell me what they are?"*
5. **You:** reply *"Salary is NPR 50,000 and email is hr@company.com"*.
6. **AI Agent:** Combines your new message with its Memory of the image. It now has all 6 items!
7. **Custom Tool:** The AI automatically triggers the `SaveToSupabase` tool, passing it the perfect, complete JSON.
8. **Supabase:** Inserts the job onto your live website.
9. **Telegram Action:** Sends you: *"Done! The job is now live on Desire Job Hub."*
